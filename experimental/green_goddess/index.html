    Green Goddess Dip¶

    info¶

    • About 10 minutes
    • 2 cups

    ingredients¶

    2 teaspoons  dried tarragon
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon water
1 medium clove garlic — chopped
1/4 cup  mayonnaise
1 1/2 cups strained Greek-style yogurt or sour cream
1/4 cup  picked fresh chervil leaves (or parsley, if not available)
1 anchovy fillet — rinsed and dried
1/4 cup  finely sliced chives
2 teaspoons  worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon hot sauce, such as Franks
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

    steps¶

    1. In a small bowl, combine the tarragon, lemon juice, garlic, and water. Allow those ingredients to sit for 15 minutes.
    2. Using a blender, process the tarragon mixture, mayonnaise, yogurt, chervil, and anchovies until smooth. Scrape down the sides of the blender jar as necessary.
    3. Transfer to bowl, stir in the chives, worcestershire, hot sauce, and season with salt and pepper. Chill about an hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
    4. Can be stored, covered, in the refrigerator up to 1 week.

    notes¶

    • ¾ cup mayonnaise (original amount)
